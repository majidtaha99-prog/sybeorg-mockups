What drivers can send before arrival

  • Vehicle year / make / model
  • Main symptom and when it started
  • Warning lights or codes if available
  • Preferred call-back time

What gets confirmed by the shop

  • Real services and specialties
  • Hours, parking, and drop-off process
  • Photos from the actual location
  • Any warranties, certifications, or review proof
