# SYBEORG Video Ads + Business Commercial Offer Strategy

Updated: 2026-06-19  
Owner: Hermes for Stark / SYBEORG

## Priority

High. This should be treated as a premium, high-ticket expansion of the current website/mockup outreach engine.

SYBEORG can now lead with: **we do not just build the page — we can create the commercial/video ad that sends customers to it.**

## Core Positioning

> Most local businesses either have no professional video or only random phone footage. SYBEORG can give them a polished commercial, cut it into ads, and connect it to a landing page built to turn attention into calls.

This is stronger than selling “a video” by itself. The package should be sold as a customer-acquisition asset:

1. Professional commercial/video shoot
2. Short-form ad cuts for Facebook/Instagram/TikTok/YouTube
3. Landing page or website refresh
4. Optional paid ad launch/management
5. Optional monthly content retainer

## Recommended Offers

### 1. The Local Commercial Starter
Best for: trades, auto shops, restaurants, gyms, med spas, salons, nonprofits, realtors, clinics.

Deliverables:
- 1 half-day shoot
- 1 polished 45–60 sec brand/commercial video
- 3 short vertical cuts, 10–20 sec each
- 5–10 photo stills from shoot
- Basic captions/subtitles
- Usage for website + organic social

Price target: **$1,500–$2,500**

### 2. The Video Ad Launch Package
Best for: businesses that need leads/bookings now.

Deliverables:
- Everything in Starter
- Dedicated landing page matched to the video offer
- 5 ad hooks/copy variations
- Meta/Google/YouTube launch-ready ad assets
- Tracking/CTA plan

Price target: **$3,500–$6,500**

### 3. The Monthly Growth Content Retainer
Best for: recurring revenue and businesses that constantly need content.

Deliverables monthly:
- 1 shoot window per month
- 4–8 reels/short ads
- 10–20 edited clips/photos
- Monthly promo or seasonal offer refresh
- Optional ad management add-on

Price target: **$1,200–$4,000/mo** depending on scope.

## Bundle / Separate Logic

### Include as an upsell in most outreach
Use one paragraph in every relevant website mockup email:

> We also have a commercial/video production side, so if you ever wanted a polished 30–60 sec business commercial or short video ads, we can pair the page with a real shoot and turn it into an ad campaign.

### Push as the primary hook for high-fit businesses
Video-first outreach should target:
- Restaurants / cafes / food trucks
- Gyms / personal trainers / martial arts
- Med spas / salons / barbers
- Auto shops / detailers / dealerships
- Contractors with visual before/after work
- Real estate agents / property managers
- Event venues / wedding vendors
- Nonprofits / churches / community orgs
- Clinics / dentists / chiropractors
- High-ticket home services: roofing, remodeling, landscaping, waterproofing

### Do not overemphasize video for emergency trades unless paired to ads
For plumbers/HVAC/emergency services, video is still useful, but the strongest pitch is:
- trust-building owner/team commercial
- seasonal tune-up ad
- recruiting video
- before/after proof
- landing page + ads conversion system

## Discovery Call Questions

1. What service/product do you most want more customers for?
2. Do you have any professional video right now?
3. Where would the video be used: website, social, ads, TV, internal, all?
4. Who needs to be on camera: owner, team, customers, nobody?
5. What locations can we shoot: storefront, job site, studio, client home, vehicle fleet?
6. Do you need one main commercial or ongoing short-form content?
7. Do you want us to run ads with it or just deliver assets?
8. What would one new customer/booking/job be worth?
