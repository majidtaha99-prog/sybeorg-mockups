## Campaign thesis

Lead with a concrete commercial concept, not generic “we do video.” Best pitch:

> We can turn one professional shoot into a polished 30–60 sec business commercial, short vertical ad cuts, website clips, and a landing page campaign.

## Top prospect categories

1. Restaurants/cafes — food, atmosphere, catering, events.
2. Gyms/fitness — transformation, classes, community, member stories.
3. Med spas/salons/barbers — before/after, premium service education.
4. Auto/detailing/dealers — inventory, trust, before/after, service promotions.
5. Contractors/remodelers/landscapers — before/after proof and high-ticket jobs.
6. Venues/hospitality/real estate — walkthroughs, events, listing/showcase videos.

## Outreach angle by vertical

### Restaurants

### Gyms
Subject: quick member-growth video idea

Hook: “Show the facility, coaches, and member experience before someone ever walks in.”

### Med spa / salon
Subject: video idea for {{service}} leads

Hook: “Premium services sell better when people can see the experience and trust the provider.”

### Auto
Subject: short video ads for inventory/service

Hook: “Vehicle walkarounds + service trust videos can become paid ads and website content.”

### Contractors
Subject: before/after commercial idea

Hook: “Your best proof is visual. We can turn finished projects into a credibility campaign.”

### Venues / real estate
Subject: walkthrough video idea

Hook: “A polished walkthrough can sell the space before the first call.”
